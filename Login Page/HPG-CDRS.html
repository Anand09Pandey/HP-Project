HPG-CDRS


Himachal Pradesh Govenment, Child Death Review System( HPG-CDRS ) is a website developed by the students of Indian Institute of Information Technology,Una (IIITU), H.P. to aid the Himachal Pradesh Government Health Ministry in reviewing the rate child mortality in the state.


Mortality Rate:

At an average in HP, Newborn mortality rate is 16 per 1000 live births, IMR is 25 per 1000 live births, and Under 5 death rate is 28 per 1000 live births. In addition Still birth rate is 24 per 1000 live births. This translates to 1600 neonatal deaths (0-28 days), 900 infant deaths (1-12 months), and 300 deaths(1-5 years of age) every year. In addition, 2400 Still births every year expected. There are total 12 districts with 75 blocks and 124 delivery points in whole state.


Objectives:

  1. Real time capture of data
  2. Complete data
  3. Authentic and useful data for action and program planning
  4. Android based application, as well as web based software. The forms are available online on mobile phones, can be completed online or offline. And syn with web based portal so that information is available immediately and accurate. The defaulters may be messaged if some information is missing or incomplete/ wrong information is submitted.
  5. Login password protected, login generated for each Block.
  6. The software should be synced with 104 reported excel sheet such that the name of dead child, address, and ASHA name is automatically entered into the software.
  7. The final data in form of excel sheet be available on click of button.
